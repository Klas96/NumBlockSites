Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how NumBlocks collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our app and website.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Data: We do not require you to provide personal information to use NumBlocks.
  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve the app, such as puzzle completion rates and error reports. This data does not identify you personally.

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the NumBlocks experience.
  • To fix bugs and enhance features based on anonymous feedback.

Data Sharing

We do not share your personal data with third parties. Anonymous usage data may be used internally to improve the app.

Cookies and Tracking

Our website may use cookies for basic functionality. We do not use cookies for advertising or tracking your activity across other sites.

Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we strive to use commercially acceptable means to safeguard your information.

Your Rights

You have the right to request information about any data we may have about you, or to request deletion of your data. Please contact us if you have any questions or requests.
